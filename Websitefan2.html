What Kind of Pianos Are There?

There are three main types of Pianos
  1. Digital/Keyboards
  2. Upright
  3. Grand/Horizontal
Although they are all pianos, the richness of the tones varies dramitically between the different instruments
Below are the different kind of pianos with their Price ranges and Ranking based on quality.

Digital Piano There are two different types of Digital instrimunts: the piano and the Keyboard. The Keyboard is completely without the use of any mechanical technology while a Digital Piano contains some Mechanical technology that produces better Sound.

Upright Upright Pianos are completely mechanical but somewhat limited in their ability to produce a rich sound due to the small space that the strings are confined to. Additionally, the ability for the pianos to produce loud sound is limited because the system of strings are completely closed and can sound muffled.
Price Range : Varies based on size but is a good medium price range at: $2000-$10000
Grand/Horizontal Grand and Horizontal Pianos are the some of the best pianos in the world. They are extremely pricey
